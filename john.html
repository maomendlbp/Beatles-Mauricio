Born in Liverpool, Lennon became involved in the skiffle craze as a teenager. In 1956, he formed The Quarrymen, which evolved into the Beatles in 1960. Sometimes called "the smart Beatle" Lennon initially was the group's de facto leader, a role he gradually ceded to McCartney.
