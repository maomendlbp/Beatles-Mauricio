George Harrison

George Harrison (25 February 1943 29 November 2001) was an English musician, singer and songwriter who achieved international fame as the lead guitarist of the Beatles.

Sometimes called "the quiet Beatle", Harrison embraced Indian culture and helped broaden the scope of popular music through his incorporation of Indian instrumentation and Hindu-aligned spirituality in the Beatles.
